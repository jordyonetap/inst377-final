What is SpinRate???

The Project

SpinRate is a full-stack music review web app built as a final project by Jordan Bennett. It lets users log album reviews, displays album art, and displays scores by genre as a chart.

How It Works

First, fill out the submission form for a new album listen or for an old favorite. Once you press the "Log Review" button, your review will be saved to a Supabase database. Your anonymous review will then be visible for other users to see. The album artwork will be displayed as part of the carousel, and the score will be added to the totals by genre.
